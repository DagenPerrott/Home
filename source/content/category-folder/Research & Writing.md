A selection of my writing and research can be found here organized first by type ([[#Academic Publications]], [[#Community Reports]], or [[#Public Media & Blogs]]) which is then organized by date. 

In particular I want to highlight the work I have been able to do with Winnipeg's [South Valour Resident Association](https://south-valour-residents-association.mailchimpsites.com/) over the past several years. Together, we conducted a literature review on key components of an anti-oppressive and grassroots approach to safety ([which you can find here]([https://mra-mb.ca/publication/grassroots-anti-oppressive-approaches-to-safety/](https://mra-mb.ca/publication/grassroots-anti-oppressive-approaches-to-safety/))) and pulled together a toolkit on how other communities might be able to do the same ([which can be found here]([https://www.uwinnipeg.ca/community-safety-toolbox/](https://www.uwinnipeg.ca/community-safety-toolbox/))).
